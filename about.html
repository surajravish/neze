About NEZE

NEZE designs and delivers intelligent traffic enforcement and safety solutions for modern cities, highways and industrial campuses. Our portfolio includes ANPR systems, 2D/4D radars, lidar speed devices, interceptor vehicles and connected software platforms.

Our Mission

Driving innovation, enhancing safety – by combining field-tested hardware with powerful AI software.

What we do

  • Design and manufacture enforcement devices for Indian road conditions.
  • Provide end-to-end deployments including civil work, power and networking.
  • Integrate with state e-challan systems and custom dashboards.
  • Offer training, AMC and 24×7 support for our clients.
